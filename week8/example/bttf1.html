back to the future (1985)

synopsis

back to the future is a 1985 american science-fiction adventure comedy film[5] directed by robert zemeckis and written by zemeckis and bob gale. it stars michael j. fox as teenager marty mcfly, who is sent back in time to 1955, where he meets his future parents in high school and accidentally becomes his mother's romantic interest. christopher lloyd portrays the eccentric scientist dr. emmett "doc" brown, marty's friend who helps him repair the damage to history by advising marty how to cause his parents to fall in love. marty and doc must also find a way to return to 1985.

trivia

writers bob gale and robert zemeckis actually received a fan letter from john delorean after the film's release, thanking them for immortalizing his car.

the script was rejected 40 times before it was finally green-lit.
